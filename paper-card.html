Instructions: Download and print PDF. Make sure all prints are to scale using the 1 inch square guide. Red lines are a valley folds, Blue lines are mountain folds, and black lines are cut lines. Line up the print with cardstock and cut as needed. Use a scoring edge to score the areas to fold and coaxe the paper into the correct folded directions while clowly closing the card. With a second sheet of paper or cardstock back the card. You can paint or color the inside of the second paper to create a color shadow behind the cut. Alternatively you can color the parts of the image that stand forward. Once the pages are glued together trim the edges to make sure they line up cleanly.
